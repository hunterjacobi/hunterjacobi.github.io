About Me

 Hello! My name is Hunter, and I was born and raise in Madison Indiana, also known as the Princess of the Rivers. I have an entrepreneurial spirit, that has helped me to build a habit of life-long learning. While growing up, I was always interested in helping people. Throughout middle school, and high school, I joined student council groups, and the National Honor Society, where we volunteered locally in our community.

 While in primary schools, I took computer science class for three years, and a year of graphic design. While studying on my own time at home, I've enjoyed learning to program, and about graphic design. After graduating High School, I enrolled at Ivy Tech to receive a Business Administration Technical Certificate.

 Currently, I am enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy's Software development program, while also working two jobs. I am currently a Cashier at Big Lots, and a business development specialist at Chandler's Chevrolet.
